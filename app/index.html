BEACHES

Surf. Snorkel. Serenity.

The Capricorn Coast is renowned for its 75 kilometres of beaches and the various islands out on the bay. With only a few small towns nearby, you can easily find a beach all for yourself.

Thanks to its location, it is blessed with idyllic tropical weather all year round. It's always a good day to visit the beach.
