WILDLIFEWILDLIFE

Cooberrie Park Wildlife Sanctuary

Welcome to Cooberrie Park Wildlife Sanctuary. You can cuddle a koala, hold a crocodile, snake or lizard, play with birds, interact with playful monkeys and hand feed kangaroos.

Cooberrie Park is fun for people of all ages. It can be found just 15 minutes from the beach.
